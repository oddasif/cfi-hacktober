About HacktoberFest

HacktoberFest is DigitalOcean's annual event that encourages people to contribute to open source throughout the month of October.
As a part of HacktoberFest 2022, Code for India Foundation is organising a series of Hackathons throughout the month. This hackathon marathon is for Programmers, Developers, Innovators, creators, movers, and shakers (aka people like YOU!).
